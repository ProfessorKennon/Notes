# Craft Stick House

Here's how to build a simple, colorful **craft stick house** using your materials:

Materials Needed:
- Popsicle sticks
- Pipe cleaners
- Pom-poms
- Beads
- Colored paper
- Glue (or tape)
- Paint

Instructions:
1. **Base and Walls:**
> 	- Lay down 5-6 popsicle sticks side by side to form the floor.
	- Glue two sticks horizontally across the top and bottom to hold them together.
	- Build the walls by gluing sticks vertically, stacking them, or creating square/rectangular frames.
1. **Roof:**
	- Create two triangles with popsicle sticks for the roof's peak.
	- Attach them to the top of the walls, then glue sticks across the triangles to form the roof.
1. **Windows and Doors:**
	- Cut small rectangles from colored paper for windows and doors.
	- Decorate with beads as doorknobs and pipe cleaners as window frames.
1. **Decorations:**
	- Add pom-poms as bushes or flowers.
	- Use pipe cleaners to create vines or fences.
	- Paint the house for extra color, and glue beads on for decorative details.
1. **Assembly:**
	- Let each section dry before assembling the full house.
	- Use extra glue to secure joints and corners for stability.

Enjoy your colorful craft house!
