## World Cup
I was thinking have teams face to face in a plank loser does merkins winner gets to drop knees. Pass the die back and forth. 
**Extra Time: The Penalty Shootout (Head-to-Head)**
Split back into the two teams. It’s time for penalty kicks to decide the winner.
 Match up one guy from Team A (The Striker) against one guy from Team B (The Goalie).
 **The Roll:** Both guys roll the d20 at the same time.
 **Striker Wins the Roll (Goal!):** Team B must do 10 Hand-Release Merkins. Team A holds a plank and mocks them.
 **Goalie Wins the Roll (Save!):** Team A must do 10 Hand-Release Merkins. Team B holds a plank and mocks them.
 **Tie:** Both teams do 5 Burpees.
 Cycle through until every man has gone head-to-head. Tally the score at the end to declare the World Cup Champions!

## Frozen Four

4 cones in a square about 20 yards apart, do an exercise at each cone.  Roll the die to see what happens next.

(Warm Up) Round 1: 10 Reachers, 10 Hillbillies, 10 Don Quixotes, Motivators to 7. Calisthenics between cones. 

Round 2: 10 Merkins, 10 Lunges, 10 Plank Jacks, 10 LBCs, Mosey between cones. Roll the die - odd number - sprint around the cones, even number - double the reps next round. Repeat for 10 minutes. 

Round 3: 10 Curls, 10 Squats, 10 Overhead Press, 10 Leg Lifts, Mosey between cones. Roll the die - odd number - normal reps, even number - tempo reps. Repeat for 10 minutes. 

Round 4: 4 Manmakers, 4 Burpees, 4 Salt Shakers, 4 American Hammers (4 count), Mosey between cones. Roll the die - odd number - cut the reps in half, even number - double the reps. Repeat for 10 minutes.
