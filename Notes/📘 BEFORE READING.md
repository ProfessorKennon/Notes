📘 BEFORE READING
-What do you think this book is about?
-What do you see on the cover?
-What do you think will happen in the story?
-Do you think this will be a funny/sad/exciting book? Why?

📖 DURING READING
-What’s happening right now?
-How do you think the character feels?
-Why did they do that?
-What do you think will happen next?
-Does this remind you of anything you’ve done or seen?

📗 AFTER READING
-What was your favorite part?
-What was the problem in the story? How was it solved?
-Who was your favorite character? Why?
-If you could change the ending, what would you do?
-What did you learn from this story?

✨ BONUS QUESTION
-Can you tell me the story in your own words? -What happened first, next, and last?
