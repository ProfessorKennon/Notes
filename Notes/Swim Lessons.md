## 🏊 Swim Skill Checklist

### ✅ Level 1: Water Comfort & Safety
- ☐ **Enter/exit pool safely**  
  *Drill:* Practice using pool steps with support.  
- ☐ **Blow bubbles for 5+ seconds**  
  *Drill:* “Birthday Cake” – blow bubbles to “blow out candles.”  
- ☐ **Put face in water**  
  *Drill:* “Magic Dips” – dip nose, chin, mouth step by step.  
- ☐ **Float on back (assisted)**  
  *Drill:* “Starfish Float” – support back while relaxing.  
- ☐ **Kick legs holding wall or floaty**  
  *Drill:* “Splash Contest” – biggest kicks holding the edge.  

---

### ✅ Level 2: Independent Movement
- ☐ **Front float (5–10 sec unassisted)**  
  *Drill:* Push off wall, float with arms forward.  
- ☐ **Back float (5–10 sec unassisted)**  
  *Drill:* “Ears In” – float with ears under water.  
- ☐ **Glide 5 ft from wall**  
  *Drill:* “Superhero Glide” – arms forward, push off and coast.  
- ☐ **Retrieve object underwater**  
  *Drill:* “Treasure Hunt” – dive for toys on shallow bottom.  
- ☐ **Kick 5 ft unassisted**  
  *Drill:* Kick toward a toy without holding on.  

---

### ✅ Level 3: Beginner Swimming & Basic Strokes
- ☐ **Basic freestyle arms**  
  *Drill:* “Windmill Arms” – big arm circles on land or wall.  
- ☐ **Kick + arm coordination**  
  *Drill:* Paddle & kick to cross 5–10 ft.  
- ☐ **Swim 10–15 ft with help**  
  *Drill:* Swim toward parent or toy with gentle assist.  
- ☐ **Roll from front to back**  
  *Drill:* Swim 3 strokes, roll onto back to rest.  
- ☐ **Swim to toy**  
  *Drill:* Swim toward floating duck or target.  

---

### ✅ Level 4: Stroke Development & Endurance
- ☐ **Swim 25 ft freestyle**  
  *Drill:* Full-length swim with side breathing.  
- ☐ **Swim 25 ft backstroke**  
  *Drill:* Swim with arms and kick in rhythm.  
- ☐ **Side breath while swimming**  
  *Drill:* Bubble-bubble-breathe pattern.  
- ☐ **Tread water (10 sec)**  
  *Drill:* “Helicopter Arms” + flutter kick.  
- ☐ **Underwater swim 5 ft**  
  *Drill:* Glide through a hoop or under parent’s legs.  

---

### ✅ Level 5: Competent Swimmer
- ☐ **Swim 50 ft freestyle**  
  *Drill:* Swim two lengths of shallow pool with breathing.  
- ☐ **Swim 50 ft backstroke**  
  *Drill:* Backstroke from wall to wall.  
- ☐ **Tread water 30+ sec**  
  *Drill:* Tread while singing or holding toy.  
- ☐ **Underwater swim 10+ ft**  
  *Drill:* Swim distance underwater to retrieve object.  
- ☐ **Float on back calmly**  
  *Drill:* Float and count/sing without fear.  
- ☐ **Know how to get help**  
  *Drill:* Practice yelling, floating, and waving.  

---

## 📅 8-Week Swim Lesson Plan

**Week 1: Water Comfort**
- Safe entry/exit  
- Blow bubbles  
- Face dips  
- Assisted back float  

**Week 2: Floating & Gliding**
- Front/back floats  
- Glide 5 ft  
- Bubble games  
- Wall kicks  

**Week 3: Independence**
- Front float (unassisted)  
- Retrieve toys  
- Glide & kick  
- Back float  

**Week 4: Coordination**
- Freestyle arms  
- Swim to parent  
- Kick + scoop arms  
- Roll front to back  

**Week 5: Stroke Basics**
- Swim 10–15 ft  
- Side breathing  
- Backstroke float  
- Underwater 5 ft  

**Week 6: Endurance**
- Swim 25 ft freestyle  
- Swim 25 ft backstroke  
- Tread water 10 sec  
- Treasure dive  

**Week 7: Distance & Safety**
- Swim 50 ft freestyle  
- Tread water 30 sec  
- Float on back  
- Underwater 10+ ft  

**Week 8: Mastery & Review**
- Freestyle with side breathing  
- 50 ft backstroke  
- Treading test  
- Water safety game (float + yell + wave)
